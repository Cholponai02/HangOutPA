# HangOut

## Overview

HangOut is a cross-platform mobile application developed with .NET MAUI that helps teenagers and young adults spend less time online and engage more in real-world social activities.

The project was created in response to a growing problem of excessive screen usage among teenagers. Interviews conducted during the project showed that many young people spend several hours a day on social media, often feeling bored, disconnected, and struggling to organize offline activities with friends.

HangOut acts as a bridge between online communication and real-life interaction by helping users discover activities, organize events, gather participants, and coordinate group outings.

---

## Problem Statement

Many teenagers and young adults:

* Spend excessive time on social media.
* Struggle to find activities outside the home.
* Have difficulty organizing group events.
* Frequently experience canceled plans.
* Do not know about local activities and opportunities.

As a result, social interaction increasingly happens through screens instead of face-to-face communication.

---

## Solution

HangOut is designed as an "offline-first social platform".

The application encourages users to move from online interaction to real-world experiences by providing tools for event organization, group communication, and activity discovery.

---

## Main Features

### Event Feed

Users can browse and discover upcoming activities and events.

### Groups and Communities

Create private or public groups based on shared interests.

### Event Management

* Create events
* Join events
* Manage participants
* Track attendance

### Polling System

Allow group members to vote on activities, locations, or schedules.

### Crowdfunding Pot

Collect contributions for shared event expenses such as:

* Sports activities
* Food and drinks
* Equipment rental
* Tickets

### Comments and Interaction

Participants can discuss event details directly inside the application.

### Offline Verification

Users can check in and confirm participation in real-life activities.

## Project Goal

The goal of HangOut is not to increase screen time but to encourage meaningful offline experiences and help young people build real social connections.

Our vision is simple:

> Use technology to help people spend less time on technology.
